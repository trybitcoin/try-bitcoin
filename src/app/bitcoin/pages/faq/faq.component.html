Frequently Asked Questions Who invented Bitcoin?

In 2009 an anonymous online entity called Satoshi Nakamoto shared the Bitcoin White Paper on an online mailing list. This only nine page long paper explains how Bitcoin works on a technical level. Some month later he released the first working version of the Bitcoin software. Satoshi disappeared in 2011 and has never been online since. Since then hundreds of developers worldwide have joined the project and continued to work on and improve Bitcoin.
