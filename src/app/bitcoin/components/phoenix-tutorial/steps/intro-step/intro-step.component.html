Bitcoin is an open-source software that since 2009 has enabled the exchange of an entirely new form of money over the internet.

As set out in the rules of the software, only 21 million bitcoins, divisible into many more smaller units, will ever be created.

Bitcoins are directly transferred from person to person without the need of financial institutions.

Try-bitcoin.com teaches you how to send & receive payments in under 5 minutes.

After you complete the tutorial, try-bitcoin.com shows you the best ways to buy bitcoin for your country.
