A wallet is device or software application that allows you to send and receive Bitcoin payments.

Wallet Types

There are three different types of wallets:

1. Web Wallets are custodial wallets offered by companies. The usage is highly discouraged because they can get hacked or steal your money.

2. Mobile & Desktop Wallet are applications that you install onto your phone or your computer. They make frequent transactions easy while keeping the bitcoins fully under your control.

3. Hardware Wallets are specific devices that offer the most secure way to store Bitcoin. They are best suited for secure, long-term storage.

Wallet Recovery

Wallets can either be custodial or not custodial.

Non custodial wallets usually ask the user to write down a recovery seed (12 to 24 words).

If your phone or computer breaks this is the only way to recover your money.
